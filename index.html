About Us

Serving Healthcare Excellence

Dedicated to public welfare since 1968, providing accessible and affordable healthcare services to the community.

Maharshi Raman Diagnostic Center

Since 1968, Banipark Dharmarth Sansthan has been dedicated to public welfare. Operating the Maharshi Raman Diagnostic Center since 2000, we provide comprehensive medical diagnostic services at SMS Hospital-equivalent rates. Our facility features state-of-the-art equipment, NABL & ILAC accreditation, and a team of experienced doctors and trained technicians.

We offer expert medical consultations across 13+ specialties including General Medicine, Pediatrics, Gynecology, ENT, Ophthalmology, Orthopedics, and more, ensuring accessible healthcare for all.
