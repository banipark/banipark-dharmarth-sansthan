Banipark Dharmarth Sansthan has been working for public welfare since the year 1968. With the aim of providing medical diagnostic facilities to the general public, the organization has been operating the Maharshi Raman Diagnostic Center since August 2000. Through this center, citizens are provided with medical diagnostic services at SMS Hospital–equivalent rates using the latest advanced technological equipment, along with senior doctors and trained technicians.
Recently, based on the results of the pathology tests provided by the institution, it has received accreditation from the National Accreditation Board for Laboratories (NABL) and ILAC (International Laboratory Accreditation Corporation). Along with this, the institution also provides medical consultation from various experts.
At present, the institution’s OPD offers medical consultation services by highly experienced doctors in the fields of Dentistry, Pediatrics, General Medicine, General Surgery, Diabetic Foot Care, Gynecology, ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat), Ophthalmology, Homeopathy, Physiotherapy, Sujok Acupressure, Naturopathy, and Orthopedics, delivering essential healthcare services to those in need.
