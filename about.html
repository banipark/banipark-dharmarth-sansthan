Our Story

Banipark Dharmarth Sansthan

Dedicated to public welfare and accessible healthcare for over five decades

Banipark Dharmarth Sansthan has been working for public welfare since the year 1968. With the aim of providing medical diagnostic facilities to the general public, the organization has been operating the Maharshi Raman Diagnostic Center since August 2000.

Through this center, citizens are provided with medical diagnostic services at SMS Hospital–equivalent rates using the latest advanced technological equipment, along with senior doctors and trained technicians. Our commitment to affordable healthcare ensures that quality medical services are accessible to everyone in the community.

Recently, based on the results of the pathology tests provided by the institution, it has received accreditation from the National Accreditation Board for Laboratories (NABL) and ILAC (International Laboratory Accreditation Corporation). This recognition reflects our dedication to maintaining the highest standards of quality and accuracy in diagnostic services.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality medical diagnostic services to the general public, ensuring that advanced healthcare facilities are available to everyone at SMS Hospital-equivalent rates.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and accessible healthcare diagnostic center in Jaipur, recognized for excellence in medical services, patient care, and community welfare.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, excellence, and accessibility guide everything we do. We believe that quality healthcare is a fundamental right, not a privilege.
